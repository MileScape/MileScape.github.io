Motivation

Why build MileScape?

Most apps focus only on quantitative data—distance, pace, calories—ignoring emotional value. Users struggle to maintain long-term commitment.

MileScape shifts running from passive tracking to an immersive experience, turning every run into a visual journey of personal growth.
